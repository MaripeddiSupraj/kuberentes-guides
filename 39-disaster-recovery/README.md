# Disaster Recovery - Business Continuity

Kubernetes disaster recovery strategies for ensuring business continuity and data protection.

## Learning Path

### [01-basic](./01-basic/) - DR Fundamentals
- Backup and restore strategies
- Multi-region deployments
- Recovery time objectives (RTO)
- Recovery point objectives (RPO)

### [02-intermediate](./02-intermediate/) - Advanced DR Planning
- Automated failover
- Cross-cluster replication
- DR testing procedures
- Data consistency

### [03-advanced](./03-advanced/) - Enterprise DR Management
- Multi-cloud DR strategies
- Compliance requirements
- Cost optimization
- DR orchestration

## DR Components

- **Backup** - Data and configuration backup
- **Replication** - Real-time data sync
- **Failover** - Automatic service switching
- **Recovery** - Service restoration procedures
